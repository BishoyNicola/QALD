Task 3: Hybrid question answering

 

Full description: qald-4.pdf (Last updated: April 1, 2014)

 

Dataset

  • DBpedia 3.9 with English abstracts

 

SPARQL endpoint

  • http://vtentacle.techfak.uni-bielefeld.de:443/sparql

 

Training data

The training data comprises 25 English questions, annotated with corresponding SPARQL queries and the answers that these queries retrieve. All of the questions require the integration of information from both the structured (RDF) data available in DBpedia and the free text available in the English abstracts.
